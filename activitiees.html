Activities

    Camp Snoopy Activity Program

    Celebrate the launch of Camp Snoopy on Apple TV+ by learning how to be a Beagle Scout! This program guides you through activities to fill up your very own Beagle Scout Handbook with merit badges. You can use this booklet at camp or in your own backyard to get outside and learn the importance of working together and trying new things.

    Join the Mission

    Everyone’s favorite beagle went to space as the Zero Gravity Indicator for the Artemis I mission! To celebrate his incredible journey to the moon and back, use this activity sheet to design Snoopy’s Mission Patch. Every mission to the moon has its own special badge filled with meaningful symbols and colors. Now it’s up to you to create your very own!

    Journey to Space

    Let your imagination take flight with this Journey to Space activity sheet! If you had the opportunity to go to space like Snoopy did on the Artemis I mission as the Zero Gravity Indicator, what would you want to see, explore, or do? Maybe you’d want to walk on the moon, float in zero gravity, or search for signs of life on Mars! Gaze up at the sky, daydream just a bit, and write about your ideal journey to space.

    Peanuts Lesson Plans

    Snoopy, Charlie Brown, and the Peanuts Gang keep kids engaged and entertained while sharpening STEM, Language Arts, and Social Studies skills. These free resources, created for students ages 4–13, are available in 11 languages.

    Peanuts Coloring Sheets

    Everyone can enjoy the relaxing, creative fun of coloring pages featuring Snoopy, Charlie Brown, and their friends. All pages are free to download.

    Charlie Brown's Caring Tree Activity Sheet 

    Show how kindness grows by creating your very own Charlie Brown Caring Tree! From telling friends and family you love them to donating clothes, celebrate the many ways we can take care of each other.
